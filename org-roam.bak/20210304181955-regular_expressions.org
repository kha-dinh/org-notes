#+title: Regular Expressions

 - A regular expression can be used to denote [[file:20210304182544-regular_language.org][Regular Language]]
   + \varepsilon: a regular expression denote set {\varepsilon} or L(\varepsilon)
   + x and y denotes L(x) and L(y)
     - x|y : L(x) \cup L(y)
     - xy : L(x)L(y)
     - x* : L(x)*
