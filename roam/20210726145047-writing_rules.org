#+title: Writing Rules

* Ommiting article (the/a/an)
** General rules:
+ The when refer to specific person, place, thing
+ A/an means not specific.
+ Can't count -> no article
+ /Some/ of something -> no article
  - /Example/: Let's have coffee (some coffee)
