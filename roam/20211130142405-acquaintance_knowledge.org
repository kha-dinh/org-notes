#+title: Acquaintance Knowledge

Is a [[id:4c73322d-6dc1-404a-9a6c-d48da700709a][Knowledge]] acquired by the familiarity with a person, thing, place, often obtained through experience.
Acquaintance knowledge is often associated with objects, as apposed to [[id:b4390d3d-82a0-45b5-ae6a-7cf2dcf7bf8e][Propositional Knowledge]] with facts
For example,
+ "I know Sam"
+ "I now Russell's Problems of Philosophi"
