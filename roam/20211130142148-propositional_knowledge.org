#+title: Propositional Knowledge

The [[id:4c73322d-6dc1-404a-9a6c-d48da700709a][Knowledge]] of knowing the truth of the "propositions", or facts.

Is also called descriptive knowledge, as it can be expressed in a sentence, propositions.
